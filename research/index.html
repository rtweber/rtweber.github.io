CS Theory Research

During my Ph.D. program, my research focused on combinatorial approaches to questions relevant to (theoretical) computer scientists. I worked mainly on matching problems.

These days, I am not active in cutting edge theory research, though I do occasionally advise mid-career undergraduates in theory research.
